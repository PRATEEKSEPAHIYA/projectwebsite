MANDI ITENARY

Day 1 | Arrival at Barot
Upon arrival at the bus station, meet our representative and reach the campsite by 4 pm in the evening.
After having welcome tea and evening snacks, start your Barot Valley tour P\packages by exploring the valley.
Enjoy the leisure time visiting nearby places like Barot market, crop fields or you can take a peaceful walk amidst the cedar forest and spend some quality time near the Uhl river.
Head back to the campsite for dinner.
Enjoy the first campfire of your Barot Valley Packages and then head to your tent for a sound sleep.

Day 2 | Trekking in Barot
Wake up and have breakfast, today we take you to Barot village.
Where you will witness the Himalayan culture which also challenges you for a short trek from the thick forest to Jalan Village.
After a halt, we will take you for Trout fishing, the most popular activity of Barot Valley tour package.
Reach back to the campsite for Lunch and relax for a little while.
Head to the Uhl river to experience various adventure activities offered by Barot valley tour package like river crossing, rock climbing and rappelling.
Return to the campsite, and spend the evening at your leisure.
After having dinner, enjoy the campfire and have an overnight stay.

Day 3 | Head towards Mandi
Will be 3hrs drive take hotel rest for some time
Go to local market , see Victoria bridge and Shiv ji's temple located at heart of mandi
Take rest and next day explore new places

Day 4| Local sight seeing
Location 1: Rewalsar Lake:- Rewalsar Lake or Tso Pema as the Tibetan Buddhists call it, is sacred to Buddhists, Hindus, and Sikhs alike. Lying 24km south-west of Mandi, Himachal Pradesh.
Tso Pema translates to Lotus Lake, and legend has it that the then king of Mandi tried to burn alive Saint Padmasambhava known as Guru Rinpoche to the Tibetans).
But instead of being burnt alive, the saint was found sitting on a lotus in the middle of a newly formed lake.
Location 2: Tso-Pema Orgyen Heru-Kai Nyingmapa Gompa:- Tso-Pema Orgyen Heru-kai Nyingmapa Gompa is a revered Tibetan Buddhist Monastery located on the south-western bank of the Rewalsar lake.
Location 3: Drikung Kagyu Gompa:- Rewalsar Lake is the Drikung Kagyu Gompa, a Tibetan-style Buddhist Monastery.
It is painted in red and purple, with a central temple which houses a large Shakyamuni statue, and a Padmasambhava statue to the left. It was established in 1179 by Kyobpa Jigten Sumg.
Drikung Kagyu is one of the eight heredities of the Kagyu Schools of Tibetan Buddhism. Presently, it is headed by two Rinpoches- His Holiness Drikung Chetsang and His Holiness Drikung Chungstang.

Day 5 | Head towards Prashar Lake
Report at Panarsa in a homestay at 9:00 AM where you can get freshened up.
Grab a delicious breakfast to ease your cravings.
Leave for Jwalapur at around 10:30 AM, which is 20 km from Panarsa in a shared cab.
Reach Jwalapur at around 11:30 AM and begin your trekking adventure.
The Prashar Lake trek is 8 km long and it will take around 4 hours to reach the beautiful Prashar Lake.
The route to Prashar lake is filled with lush greenery, numerous waterfalls, and small streams.
Get served with packed lunch on the way in the midst of the trekking trail at around 2:00 PM.
After trekking to Prashar lake, you are free to visit the Prashar Rishi temple and enjoy the views of the Himalayan ranges. Do not miss out on capturing the Himalayan silhouette during sunset.
Enjoy the delicious dinner and while sitting around a bonfire under the sky full of stars if the weather permits.
Overnight stay in camps near the lake. Retire for the day.

Day 6 | Prashar Lake to Jwalapur to Panarsa; Breakfast at the Lake
Parashar Lake Start your day by capturing the 360° view of Himalayan ranges and savour the memory forever.
After having your breakfast at around 8:30 AM near the lake.
Start descending down to Jwalapur at around 9:30 AM.
Reach Jwalapur by 12:30 PM and travel back to Panarsa from Jwalapur in a shared vehicle.
Lunch will be served to you at Panarsa homestay around 1:30 PM.
End your journey around 3:00 PM after having lunch with many great memories.

Day 7 | Make a towards your home destination
Take your drive and enjoy return back journey.
