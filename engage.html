Beas Kund

The Beas Kund is a high altitude alpine lake in the Himalayas from which the river Beas originates. Treks to Beas Kund from Manali is very popular. Whether you are a pro or a beginner, this moderate trek of 1-2 days is for everyone. As you proceed from Manali along Beas, you will witness nature’s beauty in the perfect combination of the lush green slopes, colorful wildflowers, and the fresh mountain air. This trek rewards you with spectacular views of the lofty peaks of Pir Panjal Range and the emerald green meadows in Dhundi and Bakarthach. Situated in a pocket at the side of a mountain, Beas Kund remains concealed from your sight until you reach your destination. Once there, behold the magnificent view of the royal blue waters of the Kund. Dip your feet in the water and get rid of all your tiredness. This mountain pool connects to the Hindus from the legendary days. According to the epics, Sage Vyas, the author of Mahabharata, used to take his daily bath in this pristine blue lake.
