DALHOUSIE-CHAMBA ITENARY

Day 1 | Arrival at Dalhousie
Dalhousie Khajjiar tour package commences from Pathankot. Upon your arrival at Pathankot railway station you will meet our representative and will be transferred to Dalhousie.
As you reach, check-in at the hotel and have some rest, later head out to explore this tinsel hill station.
Dalhousie Khajjiar tour packages invite you to visit the colonial-era buildings like St. Francis and St. John’s churches, built in the 1800s.
Later visit the Dainkund peak and spend some quality time at Chamera lake.
Head back to the hotel for an overnight stay as the first day of your Dalhousie Khajjiar tour plan ends here.

Day 2 | Excursion To Khajjiar

On the second day of your Dalhousie Khajjiar tour plan, enjoy your breakfast and drive to Khajjiar for an excursion.
After reaching Khajjiar, spend the day exploring the popular spots of this place, start by visiting the Khajjiar lake, situated amidst the forests of cedar trees.
You can enjoy various adventure sports here like Paragliding, horse-riding, and zorbing.
A visit to the Kalatop Wildlife sanctuary will leave you spellbound. You will witness a far stretched landscape of Theodor forests with fresh water streams and wild animals like black bear, Himalayan black marten, barking goral, leopard, wild cats, deer and serow in their natural habitat.
After the visit, travel back to dalhousie. Have dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel as the sun sets on the second day of your Dalhousie Khajjiar tour packages.

Day 3 | DALHOUSIE – CHAMBA (60 KMS DRIVE)
Morning after breakfast proceed to Chamba via Khajjiar.
See the beauty of Khajjiar deodar forest and hills, after arrival at Chamba check-in the hotel and visit Lakshmi Narayan Temple, Rang Mahal and more.
Later back to hotel for Dinner & Overnight stay.

Day 4 | CHAMBA LOCAL TOUR
Next day morning post breakfast transfer to local tour of Chamba and visit Manimahesh Peak, Champavati Temple, Chamunda Devi Temple and back to hotel for Dinner & Overnight stay.

Day 5 | CHAMBA – PATHANKOT (120 KMS DRIVE)
In the morning after breakfast check-out from the hotel and head towards your home location.
