SANGLA CHITKUL ITENARY

Day 1 | Arrival in Shimla
You will be coming across the representative local guide who will assist you in the transfer to Shimla on a drive.
Soon after you arrive in Shimla, check-in the hotel and stay overnight after having dinner..

Day 2 | Shimla to Sangla
After having breakfast, you will be departing for Sangla on a guided drive where you will come across Kufri, Fagu and also Narkanda.
Witness the mighty river Satluj after which you will check-in the hotel and take rest. In the evening you can witness the river valley of Baspa that is elevated at a height of 9000 ft, considered as one of the lovely valleys in the Mountain ranges.
You can admire the beautiful apple orchards, and the rich culture of the people out there! Stay overnight after having dinner.

Day 3 | Sangla to Chitkul
Eat the morning breakfast and start for a drive to Kalpa that takes 4 hours at the most for covering 49 km on road.
Soon after you arrive in Kalpa, you will check-in the hotel and take rest.
Later, you can pay a visit to the Buddhist Gompa that is known as the Hu Bu Lan Kar, post-lunch, Stay overnight after your return to the hotel

Day 4 | Kalpa local sightseeing
Post having the morning breakfast, you will start visiting the sightseeing places awaiting you in Kalpa like the Narayan Nagini Temple that has the shrines of Hindu,
Buddhist and other religious deities followed by an excursion to the Roghi Village to experience the Pahari life.
Visit the highest post office in the world and the last dhaba of INDIA
Later return to the hotel and stay overnight after dinner!

Day 5 | Kalpa to Shimla
Have breakfast in the morning and start for Shimla from Kalpa via Sarahan that is another beautiful place that you can stop by, en-route.
Check-in the hotel that is pre-booked and stay overnight post having dinner!

Day 6 | Departure from Shimla
Wake up to the last day's morning breakfast of the trip where you will come across a guided drive
