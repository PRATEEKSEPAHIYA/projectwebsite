KILLAR TO KISHTWAR ITENARY

Day 1 | DELHI-MANALI | 222 – 2050 MTS ASL | 538 KMS
Start from Delhi on your bike or bus
Reach Manali as per your convinience

Day 2 | MANALI – SISSU | 2050 – 3120 MTS ASL | 85 KMS
Meet our team, get briefed about the trip, receive/rent bikes if applicable.
Reach Sissu via Rohtang La, Khoksar. Explore Sissu
Overnight stay in Sissu after dinner

Day 3 | SISSU-UDAIPUR | 3120-2742 MTS ASL | 70 KMS
Have lavish breakfast at the stay. Start trip towards Udaipur
Reach Udaipur via Tandi
Overnight stay in Udaipur after dinner

Day 4 | UDAIPUR-KILLAR | 2742 – 2591 MTS ASL | 80 KMS
Have lavish breakfast at the stay. Start the trip towards Pangi valley
Reach Killar via Chindi
Overnight stay in Killar after dinner

Day 5 | KILLAR-SACH PASS-KILLAR | 2591-4400-2591 MTS ASL | 121 KMS
Have breakfast at the stay, start towards the ultimate mountain top of Pangi valley Sach Pass
Reach Sach Pass top and head back to Killar
Overnight stay in Killar after dinner

Day 6 | KILLAR-KISHTWAR | 3810-1638 MTS ASL | 121 KMS
Have breakfast at the stay, start towards Kashmir via the cliffhanger road
Reach Kishtwar
Overnight stay in Kishtwar after dinner

Day 7 | KISHTWAR-PATNI TOP | 1638-2024 MTS ASL | 120 KMS Have breakfast at the stay, start towards Patni Top Reach Patni Top Overnight stay in Patni Top after dinner

Day 8 | KAZA-LOSAR-CHANDERTAL | 2024 – 234 MTS ASL | 281 KMS Have breakfast at the stay
