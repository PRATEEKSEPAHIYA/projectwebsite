SHIMLA KASAULI ITENARY
Day 1 | Chandigarh and Kasauli; Visit the Rock Garden and check out the Gilbert Nature Trail
Arrive in Chandigarh and set out to sightsee the famous Rock Garden, known for its numerous sculptures made using a variety of different discarded waste
Drive down to Kasauli and check-in at the homestay/resort
Post a hearty lunch, visit the Gilbert Nature Trail and explore the flora and fauna here
Return back to the homestay and enjoy an overnight stay

Day 2 | Shimla; Hike to Jakhoo Hill
After breakfast, visit the Monkey Point and the Kasauli market
Drive down to Shimla then Visit the Mall Road, the Ridge, Christ Church, and more
Later, head out for a hike to the Jakhoo Hill. At a height of 8000 ft, it is the highest peak that offers a beautiful view of the town and of the snow-covered Himalayas.
Visit the Jakhoo Temple here Return to the stay and enjoy your evening with a bonfire followed by dinner
Overnight stay at Shimla

Day 3 | Ratnari; Stay in traditional Himachali mudhouse
Head out to Ratnari after your breakfast
Check-in to the unique home here with mud-plastered walls sitting bang in the middle of the apple orchard
Explore the orchard, learn to pluck, sow and farm
Enjoy some yoga against the beautiful backdrop
Check out the nearby trails in the deodar forest, the local villages and their cultures
Overnight stay at Ratnari

Day 4 - Chandigarh; Time to head back home Drive down to Chandigarh after a hearty breakfast and board your flight or train to the next destination or your home
